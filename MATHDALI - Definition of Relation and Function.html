DEFINITION OF RELATION AND FUNCTION

A relation is any set of ordered pairs. The set of all x-values is called the domain of the relation. The set of all y-values is called the range of the relation.
These two are the most important topics in algebra. Relations and functions are two different words that have different meanings mathematically.
All functions are relations, but not all relations are functions.
